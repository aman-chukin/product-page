ZenBook A14

Powerhouse on the Move

The particularly lightweight Zenbook A14 laptop supports mobile users in urban environments. The rounded shape of the casing and the grey and beige colour scheme, which is inspired by pebbles, ensure pleasant handling and a natural look.

Made from a magnesium-aluminium alloy with a ceramic finish, the surface is resistant to scratches and dirt. The device combines high battery performance with robust suitability for everyday use.

Featherlight. Build to last.

30% lighter, 3x stronger

At the core of the ASUS Zenbook series is ASUS’ patented Ceraluminum™ technology — an industry-first innovation refined over 4 years for precise colors, hardness, and texture. It offers unmatched scratch resistance and longevity, inspired by the natural world.

This high-tech ceramic, revered in aerospace and luxury watches, combines the lightness and hardness of ceramic with the malleability of metal, perfect for ultra-slim, durable designs.

Power unleashed. Battery unmatched.
